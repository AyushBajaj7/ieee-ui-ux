Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the team challenge? +

The challenge is open to teams of 3-5 professionals from any industry. Students in their final year of studies are also eligible to participate. Each team should have at least one member with 2+ years of relevant experience.

What is the registration fee? +

The standard registration fee is $199 per team. Early bird registrations (before March 25) receive a 20% discount. Non-profit organizations and educational institutions can apply for a reduced fee of $99.

What equipment will we need? +

All team members will need reliable internet access and a computer with video conferencing capabilities. Specific software requirements will be communicated during the orientation webinar, but most tasks can be completed using standard productivity tools.

What prizes are available? +

The winning team will receive $10,000 in cash prizes, professional development opportunities, and industry recognition. Second and third place teams will receive $5,000 and $2,500 respectively. Additionally, there are special category awards for innovation, sustainability, and social impact.

How will teams be evaluated? +

Teams will be evaluated by a panel of industry experts based on creativity, technical execution, presentation quality, and potential impact of solutions. A detailed scoring rubric will be provided during the orientation.
